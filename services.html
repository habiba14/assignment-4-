Paintings


Painting is the practice of applying paint,pigment,
color or other medium to a solid surface (called
"matrix" or "support"). The medium is commonly
applied to the base with a brush, but other
implements, such as knives, sponges, and
airbrushes, can be used. The final work is also
called a painting.





Sketches


A sketch is a rapidly executed freehand drawing that
is not usually intended as a finished work. A sketch
may serve a number of purposes: it might record some
thing that the artist sees, it might record or develop
an idea for later use or it might be used as a quick
way of graphically demonstrating an image, idea or
principle.








3D Art


3D Art is the process of developing a
representation of any surface of an object
(either inanimate or living) in three dimensions.
The product is called a 3D Art. Someone who
works with 3D models may be referred
to as a 3D artist.





Still Life


A still life is a work of art depicting mostly
inanimate subject matter, typically common place
objects which are either natural (food, flowers,
dead animals, plants, rocks, shells, etc.) or man-
made (drinking glasses, books, vases, jewelry,
coins, pipes, etc.).






Character Art Sketching


A character (sometimes known as a fictional
character) is a person or other being in a
narrative (such as a novel,play, television series,
film, or video game).The character may be
entirely fictional or based on a real-life person,
in which case the distinction of a "fictional"
versus "real" character may be made






Wall Painting


A Wall painter is an artist responsible for the
painting and decorating of houses,streets and is
also known as a decorator or house painter. The
purpose of painting is to improve the appearance
of a house.
